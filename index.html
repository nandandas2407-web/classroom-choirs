"We didn't wait for permission. We didn't wait for a budget. We just started making."

Classroom Choirs is a youth-driven cultural collective that produces short films, theatre productions, original music, and photography. Founded by students who refused to wait for the "right time," we create outside the system — inside a classroom, without permission, without budgets, without waiting.

Every production is a testament to what happens when young people decide that their voices matter enough to be heard. We are not a club. We are not a school project. We are a living, breathing cultural collective.
