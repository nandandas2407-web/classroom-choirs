"We didn't wait for permission. We didn't wait for a budget. We just started making."

Classroom Choirs is a youth-driven cultural collective that produces short films, theatre productions, original music, and photography. Founded by students who refused to wait for the "right time," we create outside the system.

Every production is a testament to what happens when young people decide that their voices matter enough to be heard.
